Welcome!

I am Gyuwon Jung, a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI). I am an active member of the Human Enhancement & Assistive Technology Research Section within the Digital Convergence Research Laboratory. Before joining ETRI, I earned my Ph.D. in the School of Computing at KAIST in 2025, under the guidance of Professor Uichin Lee, as a member of the Interactive Computing Lab.

As a researcher in the field of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), my work primarily focuses on "enhancing human understanding of data through advanced analysis techniques," with a particular emphasis on What-If Analysis. I am especially interested in developing approaches that generate practical and explainable insights from everyday-life data across various domains, including personal informatics, mobile health, and behavior change. With my research experience in Human-Data Interaction, I strive to empower users to make more rigorous and effective data-driven decisions.
